Privacy Policy

ChatRox CRM — Last updated: July 8, 2026

1. Introduction

ChatRox ("we", "our", "Platform") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your personal information in compliance with the Brazilian General Data Protection Law (LGPD - Lei 13.709/2018).

2. Data Collected

2.1. User-provided data

  • Full name, email address, phone number, company data (CNPJ), access credentials

2.2. Automatically collected data

  • IP address, browsing data, access logs, device information

2.3. Client data (service)

  • Phone number, name, message content, interaction data

4. Data Sharing

We share data only with: infrastructure providers (hosting, storage), WhatsApp/Meta (for message delivery), and legal authorities (when required by law). We do not sell personal data.

5. Storage and Security

Data is stored on secure servers in Brazil. We implement encryption in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest, with controlled access and regular backups.

6. Retention and Deletion

Data is retained while your account is active. After account closure, data is kept for 90 days for recovery, then anonymized or permanently deleted.

7. Your Rights (LGPD)

  • Confirmation and access: Know what data we process
  • Correction: Correct incomplete or outdated data
  • Anonymization: Request anonymization of unnecessary data
  • Portability: Receive data in structured format
  • Deletion: Request data deletion (except legal obligations)
  • Consent withdrawal: Cancel previously granted authorizations
